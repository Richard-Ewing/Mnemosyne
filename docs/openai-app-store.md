## Long Description
Mnemosyne provides a model-agnostic semantic ledger that records, governs, and audits meaning over time for AI systems and their users.

Instead of storing chat history or prompts, Mnemosyne maintains a governed ledger of claims and contextual meaning that AI systems can reference across conversations and models.

By eliminating silent context loss and surfacing contradictions, Mnemosyne reduces hallucinations without changing how models reason or training on user data.

Mnemosyne is not an assistant.
It is trust infrastructure.

## Required Disclosures

### Does your app train on user data?
No.

### How is user data used?
User data is processed only to provide semantic ledger functionality, including meaning representation, conflict detection, and audit logging. All ingestion is user-initiated.

### Is data shared with third parties?
Yes, only with infrastructure providers strictly required to deliver the service (e.g., encrypted storage and semantic indexing). Data is never shared for advertising or training purposes.

### Can users delete their data?
Yes. Users can export and permanently delete all data at any time.

### Does your app act autonomously?
No. Mnemosyne does not take actions or make decisions on behalf of users. It provides reference-only context.

### Does your app behave like an AI assistant?
No. Mnemosyne is not an assistant or chatbot. It is a semantic ledger that AI systems may reference.
